With this map, I show a portion of narcotic and search and seizure arrests. Because of computational power limitations, as well as improving the readability of the map by not having too many markers, I used a sample of the arrests (index < 1000). This map helps visualize the location of narcotic-related crimes, including search and seizure due to many such instances being seizure of narcotics or paraphernalia. Through the visualization of our data, we can conclude that narcotic-related crimes appear to be more frequent in West Baltimore (a la hit TV show "The Wire").
